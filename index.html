About Me

The Internet of Things (IoT) is creating new growth opportunities and is redefining industry boundaries. I want to merge my passion for IOT and Data Science so that I make a meaningful contribution to the Fourth Industrial Revolution in Africa. My undergraduate degree in Electronic Engineering has equipped me with a foundation in embedded systems design. I honed my skills as an intern at an electronic engineering company were my duties included building, testing and debugging prototypes. I was also exposed to research and design methodologies when I was building my solar maximum power point tracker project in my final year. I enrolled at HyperionDev for a Data Science Bootcamp so that I can understand further what I can do with the Big Data generated by IoT products. My passion for IoT has been further fueled by my engaging conversations with my students in class. I was tasked with designing an IoT course for the Belgium Campus, a tertiary institution where I am currently employed. It includes an introduction to prototyping using Arduino. Its deliverance has been met with a lot of enthusiasm. I would greatly appreciate an opportunity to be mentored by industry experts and a chance to expand my skills
